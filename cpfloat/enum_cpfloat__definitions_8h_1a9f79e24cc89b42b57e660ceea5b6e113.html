Enum Documentation

enum cpfloat_rounding_t

Rounding modes available in CPFloat.

Values:

enumerator CPFLOAT_RND_NA

Use round-to-nearest with ties-to-away.

enumerator CPFLOAT_RND_NZ

Use round-to-nearest with ties-to-zero.

enumerator CPFLOAT_RND_NE

Use round-to-nearest with ties-to-even.

enumerator CPFLOAT_RND_TP

Use round-toward-+.

enumerator CPFLOAT_RND_TN

Use round-toward-.

enumerator CPFLOAT_RND_TZ

Use round toward zero

enumerator CPFLOAT_RND_SP

Stochastic rounding with proportional probabilities.

enumerator CPFLOAT_RND_SE

Stochastic rounding with equal probabilities.

enumerator CPFLOAT_RND_OD

Use round-to-odd.

enumerator CPFLOAT_NO_RND

Do not perform rounding.
